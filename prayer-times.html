Jumu'ah (Friday Prayer)

Khutbah (sermon) followed by congregational prayer every Friday. Please arrive a little early — the prayer hall fills up quickly.

Exact Khutbah time: to be confirmed on your timetable widget above, or ask at the mosque / contact us.

Ramadan & Taraweeh

During Ramadan we hold nightly Taraweeh prayers and host community iftars. See our dedicated Ramadan page for the season's timetable and how to take part.

Good to know

Visiting for Prayer

  • Wudu (ablution) facilities and shoe storage are available for all visitors.
  • A separate space is available for sisters' prayer — please ask at the door if you're unsure where to go.
  • New to the mosque or new to Islam? Don't hesitate to ask anyone inside for guidance — everyone is welcome.
  • For exact Iqamah times on a specific day, please contact us or call Imam Mohamed.
